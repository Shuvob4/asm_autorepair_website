If your vehicle bounces excessively, pulls to one side, or makes clunking noises over bumps, your suspension needs attention. At ASM AUTO Repair, we inspect the entire suspension system — shocks, struts, springs, control arms, bushings, and sway bar links — to identify worn or damaged components. We use quality replacement parts and precision alignment equipment to restore your vehicle's ride comfort and handling characteristics to like-new condition.
